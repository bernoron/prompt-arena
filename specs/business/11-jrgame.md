# Game – Jump and Run – What We're Building

> **For Product Owners & Business Analysts**  
> No tech words. No file names. Just describe the user problem and solution.

---

## The Problem

Als Benutzer der Applikation will ich ein einfaches Jump and Run spielen können um mich abzulenken. 
---

## Who Benefits?

Der Nutzer verweilt länger auf der Applikation und kommt regelmässiger zurück. So sieht er oder sie zusätzliche Aspekte und die neusten Prompts und Lerninhalte. 

---

## What Are We Building?

Es gibt einen neuen Menupunkt Spiel und dort startet ein einfaches Jump and Run Spiel mit bezug zur AI 
---

## How Do We Know It Works?

- [ ] Users can see a new "Game" menu item in the main navigation
- [ ] When users click "Game", they see a simple jump and run game starting immediately
- [ ] The game has an AI theme (e.g., collecting tokens, avoiding obstacles related to AI concepts)
- [ ] Users can control the character with keyboard or touch (jump action)
- [ ] When the user dies or finishes a level, they see their score and can play again
- [ ] The game displays correctly and is playable on both desktop and mobile screens
- [ ] A user who completes a game level earns points toward their profile score

---

## What's NOT in This Release?

- No leaderboard or rankings (users see only their own score)
- No multiple game levels or progression (single level, playable repeatedly)
- No sound effects or music (game is silent)
- No achievements or badges for high scores (yet)
- No difficulty settings or customization
- No real-time multiplayer or competitive play
- Users cannot pause the game mid-play
