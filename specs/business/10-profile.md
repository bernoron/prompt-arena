# Profile & Badges – What We're Building

## The Problem

Users can't see what they've contributed to the platform. They don't know which prompts are popular, how many points they have, or what achievements they've unlocked. Without feedback, they have no motivation to improve.

## Who Benefits?

**Users** see their progress, achievements, and impact. This motivates them to keep using the platform and improve their prompt quality.

## What We're Building?

When a user clicks "Profile", they see:
- A summary card showing: name, level, points, rank
- A progress bar to the next level
- All their submitted prompts with a counter showing how many times each was used
- Special badges for achievements (like "Popular" when a prompt reaches 10+ uses)

## How Do We Know It Works?

- [ ] User sees their name, level title, and rank
- [ ] A progress bar shows how close they are to the next level
- [ ] All their submitted prompts are listed with a usage counter
- [ ] Badges appear when they achieve milestones (1st prompt, 5 prompts, 10+ uses, 4+ rating, points)
- [ ] Badges earned stay even if metrics change (can't lose them)
- [ ] Works on both mobile and desktop
- [ ] Users with no prompts see a helpful message with link to submit

## What's NOT in This Release?

- Editing profile (name, avatar)
- Analytics dashboard with trends
- Detailed usage history or export
- Comments or messaging
- Admin can't manually award badges
